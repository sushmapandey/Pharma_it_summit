India’s pharma holds a strong position in the global supply chain and play an integral role in developing the pharmaceutical industry. The need for process optimization, regulatory compliance, and improvements in the supply chain are driving investment in automation technologies across the pharmaceutical industry.

As flexibility is the key, and modern pharma industry needs to be able to re-orientate their processes according to the requirements of individual products.This industry is among the leading global producers of cost-effective generic medicines and vaccines, that supply about 20 percent of the total global demand by volume.

8th Edition Pharma Information Technology Summit is a platform that will encompass informative and interactive sessions aimed at understanding trends, strategic initiatives, opportunities and challenges in different upcoming facets of the Indian pharma industry.
